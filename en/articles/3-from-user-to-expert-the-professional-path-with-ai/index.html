From User to Expert: The Professional Path with AI

In the previous articles, you learned the basics and prompt engineering. Now it's time to transform from a regular user into a professional expert. This article shows you the path to monetization, tool selection, and continuous learning...

Techaneh Editorial

So far you've learned the basics of AI and the art of prompt engineering. But knowing these concepts alone is not enough. Today's world needs people who can not only 'talk' to AI, but also use it to create value, solve complex problems, and even earn income. In this article, we step onto the path of becoming a professional and learn how to transform from a regular user into an expert.

Step one: choose the right tool. Today's AI tool landscape is so vast that the right choice is half the battle. Each tool is designed for a specific job: ChatGPT for text generation and coding, Claude for long‑document analysis, Gemini for multimodal processing, and Midjourney/DALL‑E for professional image creation.

Step two: professional content creation. An expert uses AI to design a content strategy, not just to write text. They employ AI throughout the entire process: ideation, research, drafting, editing, and SEO optimization.

Step three: AI in business. Automating tasks, data analysis, and improving customer experience – all are possible with AI. AI tools can handle data entry, invoice processing, and even sales management.

Step four: coding with AI. Generating code, debugging, documentation, and learning new programming concepts with AI multiplies productivity. Python is recognized as the official language of the AI world.

Step five: professional learning path. Recommended roadmap: programming fundamentals (Python), mathematics & statistics, machine learning, deep learning, NLP & computer vision, and hands‑on projects.

Step six: monetization. Freelancing, developing AI tools, business consulting, teaching, and visual content production are some of the income opportunities.

AI won't replace you; but someone who uses AI will replace someone who doesn't.

Now it's your turn to take action.
