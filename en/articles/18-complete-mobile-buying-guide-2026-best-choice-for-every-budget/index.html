Complete Mobile Buying Guide 2026 | Best Choice for Every Budget

From budget phones to flagships over $2000. Find the best pick for every price range in 2026...

Techaneh Editorial

2026 is an incredible year for the mobile market. On one hand, rising component prices have pushed many new phones to higher price points. On the other, stunning advances in chips, cameras, and AI technologies have taken 2026 phones to a new level. This guide categorizes all the important phones on the market by budget and examines their specifications, strengths, and weaknesses.

Under 20 Million Toman | Budget Phones

In this budget, focus on smooth daily performance, durable battery life, and an acceptable display.

  • Samsung Galaxy A27 5G: 6.7″ 120Hz display, Snapdragon 6 Gen 3, 8GB RAM, 50MP main camera with OIS, 5000mAh battery, IP64 water resistance. Price: 12-13.5M Toman.
  • Redmi Note 15 Pro Plus: 120Hz AMOLED display, 108MP camera, 5500mAh battery, 45W charging. Price: 13-15M Toman.

20-50 Million Toman | Powerful Mid‑rangers

  • Samsung Galaxy A56 5G: 6.7″ FHD+ Super AMOLED 120Hz, Exynos 1580, 8-12GB RAM, 50MP main camera with OIS, 5000mAh battery, 45W charging, IP67, 6 years of updates. Price: 25-30M Toman.
  • vivo V60 Lite: 6.77″ AMOLED 120Hz, Dimensity 7360, 50MP Sony IMX882 main camera, 32MP selfie, 6500mAh battery, 90W charging. Price: 22-28M Toman.

50-150 Million Toman | Premium & Flagship Killers

  • Poco F8 Pro: Snapdragon 8 Elite, 6.59″ AMOLED 120Hz, 50MP camera, 6210mAh battery. Price: 50-60M Toman.
  • Apple iPhone 17: 6.3″ 120Hz ProMotion (first time on standard iPhone), A19 chip, 8GB RAM, storage from 256GB, dual 48MP camera. Price: 100-120M Toman.

150-300 Million Toman | True Flagships

  • Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra: 6.9″ Dynamic AMOLED 2X with Privacy Display, Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5, 200MP camera, 5000mAh battery, 60W charging. Price: 250-300M Toman.
  • vivo X300 Ultra: Dimensity 9500 (3nm), dual 200MP cameras, 6600mAh battery. Price: ~200M Toman.

Above 300 Million Toman | Beyond Flagship

  • Apple iPhone 18 Pro Max (Autumn 2026): First 2nm A20 Pro chip, variable aperture camera, smaller Dynamic Island. Price: over 400M Toman.
