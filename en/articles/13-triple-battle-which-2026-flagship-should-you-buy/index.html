Triple Battle: Which 2026 Flagship Should You Buy?

We compare the iPhone 18 Pro Max, Galaxy S26 Ultra, and Xiaomi 16 Ultra in every aspect: chip, camera, battery, display, and price. Which one suits you best?...

Techaneh Editorial

Three phones, three philosophies, three different paths. On one side, the iPhone 18 Pro Max with its 2nm chip and Apple's unparalleled ecosystem. On the other, the Galaxy S26 Ultra with its privacy display and 200MP camera. And in the corner, the Xiaomi 16 Ultra with its massive battery and tempting price. Which one is right for you? Let's compare these three giants point by point.

Section-by-Section Comparison

Chip & Performance: The iPhone 18 Pro Max wins. The 2nm A20 Pro is a generation ahead of its rivals. However, the performance difference won't be noticeable for most users in daily use.

Camera: The Galaxy S26 Ultra wins. The combination of a 200MP sensor with f/1.4 aperture, lens variety, and overall quality puts Samsung on top.

Battery & Charging: The Xiaomi 16 Ultra is the undisputed winner. A 6500-7500mAh battery and 100W charging leave competitors far behind.

Display: All three are excellent. Samsung has a unique trump card with Privacy Display, Apple with its smaller Dynamic Island, and Xiaomi with 3000 nits brightness.

Price: The Xiaomi 16 Ultra wins. With about a $400 difference compared to the iPhone, it offers far better value.

Final Recommendation: If you're in the Apple ecosystem → iPhone 18 Pro Max. If you're a professional photographer → Galaxy S26 Ultra. If you want the best value → Xiaomi 16 Ultra.
