iPhone 18 Pro Max: Coronation of an Emperor

The iPhone 18 Pro Max with its 2nm A20 Pro chip, variable‑aperture camera, and smaller Dynamic Island marks the biggest leap in iPhone history...

Techaneh Editorial

Every autumn, the tech world holds its breath for Apple's unveiling. But 2026 will be different. The iPhone 18 Pro Max showcases not just a phone, but a revolution. Apple is making some of the most fundamental changes in iPhone history – changes fans have dreamed of for years.

Launch: According to reports, September 2026 will see the introduction of the iPhone 18 family. Interestingly, Apple will release the Pro and Pro Max models in autumn, while standard models will be pushed to spring 2027.

Design: The iPhone 18 Pro Max will be the heaviest iPhone ever. Apple has increased thickness and weight to accommodate a larger battery and advanced hardware. The titanium frame remains, but the back glass features a new frosted design. New colours include light blue, dark grey, silver, and a special Dark Cherry. The camera bump is noticeably thicker and larger.

Display: A 6.9‑inch LTPO+ OLED with 120Hz ProMotion. But the biggest news is the 35% smaller Dynamic Island. Some sources even claim Face ID sensors will move under the display for the first time.

Chip: The heart of this phone, the A20 Pro, is Apple's first 2‑nanometer processor built by TSMC. It's about 15% faster and 30% more efficient than the previous generation. RAM is 12‑16GB and storage starts at 256GB.

Camera: A triple 48MP camera system with a variable aperture lens on the main sensor. This means the camera can adjust light intake based on ambient conditions – wide open for night, narrow for day. The telephoto lens also gets a wider aperture, and the selfie camera jumps from 12MP to 24MP.

Software: iOS 27 with a completely revamped Siri and deep AI capabilities.

Base price around $1399 – in Iran over 400 million Toman.
