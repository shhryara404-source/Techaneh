Xiaomi 16 Ultra: The Unassuming Giant Killer

The Xiaomi 16 Ultra with a 6500‑7500mAh battery, 100W charging, 200MP camera, and competitive price offers incredible value...

Techaneh Editorial

For years, Xiaomi has been trying to step out of the shadow of its larger rivals. The Xiaomi 16 Ultra, the company's latest flagship, is set to be unveiled in the first half of 2026. If the 15 Ultra was a success, the 16 Ultra aims to become legendary.

Design: Chic and modern with the Leica logo on the camera module. Colours include black and white, weight 235g. The display with ultra‑thin bezels gives the phone a striking look.

Display: A 6.85‑inch LTPO AMOLED with 2K+ resolution, 120Hz refresh rate, and a peak brightness of 3000 nits, making it one of the best displays on the market.

Chip: According to rumours, the Xiaomi 16 Ultra will be the world's first phone with the Snapdragon 8 Elite 2. 16GB RAM and 512GB internal storage.

Camera: A 200MP main sensor with continuous optical zoom. Three 50MP rear cameras and a 50MP selfie camera. The partnership with Leica brings professional‑level colour and image processing.
