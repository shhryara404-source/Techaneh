ROG Strix SCAR 18: The Ultimate Predator

ASUS breaks records with the ROG Strix SCAR 18 (320W, RTX 5090) and Zephyrus Duo (dual OLED displays)...

Techaneh Editorial

If MSI is the dragon and Apple the emperor, ASUS should be called the fearless predator. A company that pushes the boundaries of what's possible every year with its innovations. In 2026, ASUS with the ROG Strix SCAR 18 and ROG Zephyrus Duo families has once again proven it leads the gaming laptop world.

ROG Strix SCAR 18 (2026)

This laptop was unveiled on May 15, 2026, at the ROG DAY 2026 event in Guangzhou. Its beating heart is the Intel Core Ultra 9 290HX Plus processor and NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5090 graphics. But the number that truly takes your breath away is the 320W combined power of CPU and GPU. This makes the ROG Strix SCAR 18 the most powerful gaming laptop in ASUS history, even surpassing the MSI Raider 16 Max (at 300W).

The display, with 1,600 nits brightness, 100% DCI-P3 color gamut, and ELMB technology for motion blur reduction, is one of the best on the market. Additionally, the Super Edition features an AniMe Vision dot‑matrix display on the lid that shows custom animations and logos.

ROG Zephyrus Duo (2026)

If the SCAR 18 is the power predator, the Zephyrus Duo should be called the tireless innovator. This is the world's first 16‑inch gaming laptop with dual OLED displays. The combined diagonal of both screens exceeds 21 inches. Display features: 0.2ms response time, 120Hz refresh rate, 1100 nits brightness, VESA DisplayHDR True Black 1000 certification, and Delta E < 1 color accuracy. One of the most attractive features is the detachable magnetic keyboard at just 5.1mm thick with 1.7mm key travel, which also works via Bluetooth.

If you want maximum power and the best display → ROG Strix SCAR 18. If creativity and innovation are your priority and you need dual displays → ROG Zephyrus Duo.
